RULES AND REGULATIONS
The participants will be competing individually.
The first round consists of 30 MCQs which must be answered in 30 minutes (approx.).
Top 5 participants will qualify for the final round.
In case of tie(s), the participant with lesser number of incorrect answers will be selected.
The answers of the questions are from authentic sources.
Any conflict caused by the answers will be dealt by the organizers only and their decision will be final and binding.
